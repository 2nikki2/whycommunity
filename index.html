Core Platform Features

Real-Time Community Health Analytics

  • Overall Health Score — Single metric to track community vitality
  • Engagement Quality Metrics — Measure meaningful conversations, not just message counts
  • Member Journey Tracking — Onboarding success, retention rates, churn risk
  • Cross-Channel Participation — See how members engage across your server
  • Trending Topics Detection — Know what your community is talking about

AI-Powered Insights

  • Executive Summaries — Daily/weekly AI-generated reports on server health
  • Sentiment Analysis — Understand the emotional pulse of your community
  • Personalized Recommendations — AI suggests actions to improve engagement
  • Predictive Alerts — Get notified before problems escalate

Advanced Moderation Suite

  • Action Queue — AI-generated moderation tasks prioritized by urgency
  • Full Audit Logging — Complete trail of all moderator actions
  • Recurring Task Management — Automate routine moderation checks
  • Flagged User Detection — Automatic identification of suspicious accounts
  • Timeout & Ban Tracking — Historical view of all disciplinary actions

Member Intelligence

  • Influence Mapping — Identify community leaders and connectors
  • At-Risk Member Detection — Spot members likely to churn
  • New Member Tracking — Monitor onboarding success
  • Role-Based Dashboards — Different views for owners vs moderators

Campaign & Link Tracking

  • Built-in URL Shortener — Track clicks on shared links
  • Campaign Attribution — See which links drive engagement
  • Click Analytics — Referrer tracking and engagement metrics

Enterprise Security

  • OAuth 2.0 Authentication — Secure Discord-based login
  • Role-Based Access Control — Granular permissions for your team
  • Encrypted Token Storage — AES-256-GCM encryption at rest
  • Full Audit Trail — Every action logged with timestamps
