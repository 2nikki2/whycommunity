Platform Strengths

What Makes Discord
Powerful

Instant Communication

Text, voice, video, and screen sharing — all in one place, all in real-time.

Always-On Presence

Your community space exists 24/7. Members come and go as they please.

Layered Permissions

Roles let you create tiers — VIPs, moderators, newcomers — with different access.

Cross-Platform

Desktop, mobile, browser — members can connect from anywhere.

Trust & Safety

Built-In
Protection

Discord takes safety seriously — and gives you powerful tools to keep your community secure.

AutoMod

AI-powered filtering catches spam, slurs, and harmful content automatically.

Verification Levels

Require email, phone, or server membership time before members can participate.

Raid Protection

Detect and block coordinated attacks before they disrupt your community.

Moderation Tools

Timeouts, bans, message deletion, and audit logs give you full control.

Customization

Applications &
Integrations

Discord's extensive APIs and SDKs let you extend functionality with thousands of bots and custom integrations.

Custom Applications

Tailored applications and workflows designed to drive entertainment, engagement, and retention — built specifically for your community.

Social SDKs

Discord Social SDKs, linked accounts, and rich presence create seamless cross-platform experiences.

Integrations

Connect to Twitch, YouTube, Twitter, CRMs, and more. Your community, your tools.

Popular Applications: MEE6 (moderation), Carl-bot (automation), Dyno (management), Ticket Tool (support)

Engagement

Driving
Meaningful Activity

Great communities don't happen by accident. Here's what drives engagement on Discord.

Regular Events

AMAs, game nights, watch parties — give members reasons to show up.

Recognition Systems

Levels, badges, and special roles reward active members and build loyalty.

Exclusive Content

Behind-the-scenes, early access, and members-only content make people feel special.

Human Connection

Off-topic channels and casual spaces let personalities shine through.

Retention

Keeping Members
Coming Back

Getting members is one thing. Keeping them engaged for months and years is another.

Strong Onboarding

Welcome flows, intro channels, and buddy systems help new members find their footing fast.

Sub-Communities

Interest-based channels let members find their tribe within the larger community.

Consistent Value

Regular content, updates, and touchpoints remind members why they joined.

Retention Tip: Members who make a friend in the first week are 3x more likely to stay active long-term.
